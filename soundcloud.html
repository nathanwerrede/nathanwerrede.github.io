Soundcloud redesign - group project

Nhu Luong, Carlo de la Fuente, Nathan Werrede, Luke Fahmy

Why Soundcloud?

We decided to test the Soundcloud mobile application for a few reasons. The four of us all use Soundcloud, and we appreciate the music it provides us, which tends to be original and unique. However, we also feel that Soundcloud’s user experience is lacking in many ways, so we decided to try to improve it. Soundcloud has been a hot topic in the world of music streaming for quite some time now due to their uncertain future resulting from low financial gains. Throughout the last few years, the Soundcloud application has been changing and some of these newly added features seem to be a bit unpopular. We decided to conduct interviews and user tests on young adults, ranging from eighteen years to twenty-five years in age. According to publicly available data, a majority of Soundcloud users fall into this age group; therefore, these interviews and user tests should give us reliable and valuable insight into the problems most Soundcloud users face with the mobile application. We realize, however, that we are only testing on eight users (four before and four after redesign), which is not very many people, but it is enough to generate some useful insights.

Interview Plan

We used interviews to determine what component of the SoundCloud app could be of focus for our redesign.

Pre-User Testing Interview Questions

1. Name, Age, Major/Occupation

2. Music habits:

    a. How often do you listen to music?
    b. What music do you listen to?
    c. Do you stream or download?
    d. Where do you stream or download from?
    e. Do you have any previous experience/familiarity with SoundCloud?

3. For people that use SoundCloud:

    a. Do you have SoundCloud Go+?
    b. How many followers do you have?
    c. How many people are you following?
    d Do you like to make playlists?
    e. Have you discovered a lot of artists from SoundCloud?
    f. Do you use any other music streaming services?

Post-User Testing Interview Questions

1. How was your experience with Soundcloud (1-10, 1 being very unsatisfactory and 10 being very satisfactory)?

2. What did you find the most frustrating about your experience?

3. Is there anything that you would want to change on this app? (can be visual or technical)
